COMPOSITION:

pen. Beyond, deep out-of-focus warmth: terracotta planters, a brass

LIGHT:
Golden hour morning sunlight enters from the upper left at a 30° angle,
creating a glowing rim on the milk pitcher and the espresso surface.
Soft long shadows fall to the lower-right. Single light source feel.
Atmospheric haze with delicate dust particles caught in the light beam.

COLOR GRADE:
Deep espresso brown #2A1A12 in the shadows, lifted but never crushed.
Warm cream #F4ECDF on the marble and milk highlights. Antique gold
#C9A961 rim light on metal surfaces. Subtle Kodak Portra 400 film
grain. Slightly warm white balance (5400K). Lifted blacks, gentle
teal-orange contrast.

TECHNICAL:
Shot on a Sony A7R IV with an 85mm f/1.4 GM prime lens. f/1.8 aperture.
1/250s shutter for crisp milk pour with subtle motion blur. ISO 200.
Razor-thin depth of field on the milk stream and cup rim. The rest of
the frame falls into a creamy organic bokeh.

FRAMING:
16:9 horizontal landscape. The pour action sits in the right-third of
the frame. The left third and bottom-third are intentionally negative
space (soft marble + dark out-of-focus warmth) — reserved for italic
serif headline text overlay in cream. The bottom 30% of the image
gradients into deeper espresso for text legibility.

Kinfolk magazine cover × Aesop product photography × Italian fine-dining
restaurant homepage hero × Apple "Shot on iPhone" cinematic ad still.
Slow, intentional, adult, expensive, quietly confident.

no text, no watermark, no logo, no oversaturation, no HDR look, no
plastic skin, no fake hands with deformed fingers, no model posing,
no fake smile, no modern tech (no phone, no laptop, no AirPods),
no neon, no purple, no blue, no harsh studio strobe, no flat lighting,
no Pinterest filter, no Instagram preset, no overly clean studio
